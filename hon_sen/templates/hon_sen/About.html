Our objective is to have a database to every newborn that started to get vaccinated. As We

observed, mothers who carry their babies/children waiting in queue in health centers waiting to

have a shot holding a piece of booklet as their reference on what is the next vaccine to be

administered. As we talked to some mothers some of them forgot on when did their

babies got vaccinated and what type of vaccine was given, they rely too much to the baby book

they are holding. What if an unforeseen event happened ? What if the booklet got damaged or lost?

How are they going backtrack the shots? and so, I came up with the idea that it is best that

there should be an e-baby booklet as a guide not only to parents but also to healthcare
